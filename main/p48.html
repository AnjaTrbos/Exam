Pilestredet 48 Eva Balkes hus

Media and comuncation building

Pilestredet 48 is an important building within the OsloMet (Oslo Metropolitan University) campus. Located in the heart of Oslo, Norway, it serves a variety of academic and administrative purposes, contributing to the university's vibrant academic environment. This is the media buliding so this building has lots of special rooms such as photo and audio studios. This building just as the rest of them has a big auditorium and a nice little canteen.

Studios

This is the media building so having studios where students can paractise media production is essential to their eduaction. Oslomet provides 2 photo labs, two radio studios, one tv studio and ten audio rooms. Only students who study media have acess to these rooms to secure a time slot when they are available.

the canteen

The canteen of p48 is a much smaller one compared to p32. It doesnt offfer the hot homade meals that most bigger canteens offer. It doest have a salatbar or anything similar, but there is plently of sandwitches snacks and small foods for on the go. There is also an exeptional variety of energy drinks that are undoubtedly the best seller here.

The Auditorium

There are two aditoriums in this building. One of them seats 136 students and the other one is a bit smaller with only 99 seats. They are standard OsloMet audiotoriums with all of the nessecary audio visual equipment.
